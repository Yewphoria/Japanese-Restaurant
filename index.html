DEVELOPMENT


In the year 2020, our founder laid the foundation for Izakaya Euphoria. Starting with a small, committed team, we evolved into a catering company boasting a workforce of over 100 employees. The growth of our brand has been remarkable. Izakaya Euphoria is now renowned as one of Singapore's favored Japanese restaurants, a testament to the dedication of our founder, the hard work of our team, and the unwavering support of our community and diners.
